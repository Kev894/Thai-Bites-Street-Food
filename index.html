Pre-prepped Meals

Grab our chilled, pre-prepped Thai meals to heat at home. Cooked fresh, cooled quickly, clearly labelled for allergens and reheating.

  • Keep refrigerated (0–5°C). Use within 3 days or freeze on day of purchase.
  • Microwave: heat until piping hot throughout (≥75°C). Stir halfway.
  • Oven: 180°C for 18–25 mins (covered), until hot throughout.
Typical selections
  • Chicken Green Curry + Jasmine Rice
  • Pad Krapow (Basil Stir-fry) + Rice
  • Veggie Pad Thai

Allergens & Nutrition

We label allergens on our menu items. Common allergens we handle include gluten, soy, peanuts, tree nuts, eggs, fish, shellfish, sesame. While we take great care, cross-contact is possible.

Suggestions for Athletes

Bulking / Strength
  • Add extra rice or noodles (+carbs).
  • Choose protein-dense mains (chicken, prawns, tofu); add extra portion.
  • Coconut-based curries help increase calories.
Cutting / Shredding
  • Swap rice for extra veg; ask for “light oil”.
  • Choose grilled or stir-fries; avoid creamy sauces.
  • Ask for “no added sugar” where possible; watch sauces.
